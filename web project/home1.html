About Us

Krish Sustainables was founded in 2010 by Krish Patel. Krish is a passionate advocate for sustainability and is committed to making a difference in the world.

Krish Sustainables is headquartered in Bangalore, India. We have a team of experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible service.

We are committed to sustainability and social responsibility. We work with local communities to source our materials and we donate a portion of our profits to environmental charities.
