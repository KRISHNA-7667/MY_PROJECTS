SERVICES










Cocopeat For Agriculture

We produce cocopeat for various applications such as greenhouse farming, hydroponics, and potting mixes.
Which helps to enhance crop productivity, decrease water consumption, and decrease environmental harm.

Horticultural Industry

We supply cocopeat for urban gardening, nurseries, and landscaping,
that facilitates the development of healthy roots, controls soil erosion, and minimizes weed growth.

Cocopeat for Packaging Industry

We supply eco-friendly packaging materials, such as cocopeat pots, trays, and mulch mats.
Cocopeat packaging is an excellent alternative to non-biodegradable materials, such as plastic and Styrofoam.
It helps in reducing environmental pollution and waste, and promotes sustainable packaging practices.
